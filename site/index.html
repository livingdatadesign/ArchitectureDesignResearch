The future of architectural design research: An emerging niche in digital transition.

What is the future of Architectural Design Research: Responding to transition. How should Architectural design research adapt to these transitions? Based on a literature review of the DRS Conference Biennale Series spanning 23 years we reflect on the overlaps, contradictions, and synergies between architecture and design research.
